Edmund McMillen

video game designer and artist

American video game designer and artist known for his flash-game visual style.

Here is his story:

  • 1980 - 2 of march he was born in Santa Cruz, California
  • 1987 He went to Soquel High School
  • 2003 create his first video game "Carious Weltling"
  • 2004 His game Gish won Game Tunnel's 2004 Adventure Game of the Year
  • 2005 he married Danielle McMillen
  • 2009 His game Coil was nominated for the Innovation Award at the Independent Games Festival
  • 2010 together with Tommy Refens established Team Meat and released "Super Meat Boy"
  • 2011 release "The Binding of Isaac"
  • Nowadays he has some games in development such as "Super meat boy: forever" and so on...
